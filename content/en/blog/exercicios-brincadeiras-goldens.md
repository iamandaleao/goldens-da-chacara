## Daily exercise needs

Golden Retrievers are active dogs that need at least one hour of exercise per day, split into two or three sessions. This can include walks, light runs, and playtime. Regular activity helps prevent obesity, anxiety, and destructive behavior.

## Favorite activities

Goldens love retrieving objects, a trait from their history as retriever dogs. Balls, frisbees, and floating toys are excellent options. They also love water, so swimming is a great low-impact exercise for the joints. Hide-and-seek games with treats stimulate both scent work and intelligence.

## Mental exercise matters too

Beyond physical exercise, Goldens need mental stimulation. Interactive toys, puzzle games, and training new commands keep the mind engaged. Teaching tricks like "fetch", "stay", and "roll over" is fun and also strengthens your bond.

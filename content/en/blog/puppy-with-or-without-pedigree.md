## What is CBKC?

The Brazilian Confederation of Cinofilia (CBKC) is the official organization responsible for registering purebred dogs in Brazil. It is affiliated with the Fédération Cynologique Internationale (FCI), which ensures international recognition.

A pedigree issued by CBKC works as a certification of the dog's origin, providing detailed information about its lineage.

## What is a pedigree?

A pedigree is like a birth certificate for your dog. It shows the family tree, including parents, grandparents, and great-grandparents, proving that the dog is purebred.

In addition, the document indicates that the breeding was controlled, with official records and proper tracking.

## Puppy with CBKC pedigree

Choosing a puppy with a CBKC pedigree provides more security for those looking for a purebred dog.

You will have greater predictability regarding size, behavior, and physical traits. There is also a lineage history, which helps reduce risks when breeding is done responsibly.

Another important point is the higher value of the animal, as well as the possibility of participating in official shows and events.

## Puppy without pedigree

A puppy without a pedigree may look like a purebred dog, but there is no guarantee of its origin.

Without this record, it is not possible to know the lineage with certainty, which can influence appearance, behavior, and even health.

Often, the only advantage is a lower price, but this may not be worth it in the long run.

## The difference in practice

In practice, a pedigree represents security and transparency.

With it, you know exactly where your dog comes from. Without it, everything is based only on appearance.

This does not mean that a dog without pedigree cannot be loved or well cared for, but for those seeking predictability and reliability, it makes a difference.

## Is it worth it?

Yes, especially for those who want a dog with well-defined characteristics and want to avoid surprises.

A CBKC pedigree is not just a piece of paper, but a record that shows the dog's entire history and the breeder’s commitment to quality.

## Conclusion

Choosing a puppy with a pedigree means choosing more security, transparency, and quality.

More than the document itself, it is essential to choose a responsible breeder who provides proper structure, care, and follow-up from birth.
